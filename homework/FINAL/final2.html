ABOUT ME

Maria Calderon is a foodie and filmmaker from Miami, FL. She is currently a junior studying Motion Pictures at the University of Miami with a Minor in Finance. She has produced the following short films: The Cyber Stalker (2019), The Last One Standing (2019), and True Colors (2019). She invests on her part-time and owns several shares of stocks. She is a part of Delta Kappa Alpha, a prestigious film fraternity. She has worked in HP Concerts and helped organize 21 Savage homecoming concert set. She is also a member of the Cinematic Arts Commission.
