Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip cookies are one of the most delightful desserts anyone can have! They're crunchy on the outside, but yet soft on the inside. Everyone loves this as a dessert or even a snack. This recipe is for the whole family. Anyone can do it. It is easy and fun to make! All you need is to follow the steps, and gather up the ingredients.

Got Cookies?

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350ÂºF.

Step 2

Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.

Step 3

Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.

Step 4

Dissolve baking soda in hot water.

Step 5

Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Step 6

Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
